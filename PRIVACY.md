# Privacy Policy for SEO AutoFill Pro/ Roboform Free Alternative

Last updated: 2026-06-09

SEO AutoFill Pro/ Roboform Free Alternative is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for this Chrome Extension.

## 1. Data Collection & Transmission

SEO AutoFill Pro/ Roboform Free Alternative is built on a **local-first** architecture. We respect your privacy and do not collect, monitor, or store any personal data.

- **No Personal Data Collection:** The extension does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as name, email address, address, or location.
- **Local Storage:** All SEO profiles, passwords, usernames, client details, and form fields you enter are stored entirely locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API (`chrome.storage.local`).
- **No Remote Transmission:** No data is transmitted off your device. The extension does not communicate with any external servers. Your profiles stay strictly inside your browser.
- **Password Safety:** All credentials and passwords saved in profiles are obfuscated locally before being saved in database storage.

## 2. Third-Party Services

This extension does not connect to any third-party APIs, external databases, tracking systems, or analytic services (like Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.). No external network requests are initiated by the extension.

## 3. Data Sharing & Retention

- **No Data Sharing:** Since no user data is collected or transmitted off-device, no data is sold, traded, or shared with third parties.
- **Data Deletion:** All profile data saved in `chrome.storage.local` remains on your device until you manually delete a profile, clear the extension's storage in Chrome settings, or uninstall the extension.

## 4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
